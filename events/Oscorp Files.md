<h1 id="oscorp-files">OsCorp Files</h1>
<p>Microsoft Tech Club, in association with Chimera, is pleased to bring to you a unique blend of computer science and biotechnology, OsCorp Files, a bio-CTF!</p>
<h3 id="competition-problem-statement">Competition Problem Statement</h3>
<p>Solve an intriguing virtual escape room filled with bio-themed puzzles and find you way out!</p>
<h3 id="eligibility">Eligibility</h3>
<ol>
<li>Open to <strong>BPDC students of all years and disciplines</strong>.</li>
<li>Students can participate in teams of 1-3.</li>
<li>Mixed teams (non-member/member) are welcome!</li>
</ol>

</ol>
