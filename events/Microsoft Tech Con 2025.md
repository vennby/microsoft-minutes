<h1 id="microsoft-tech-con-2025">Microsoft Tech Con 2025</h1>
<p>Microsoft Tech Club is pleased to bring to you the final event of the academic semester, the Microsoft Tech Con, 2025! The Tech Con is a project expo for members and non-members to get feedback on their passion projects while also showing them off to their peers and professors! And, the best projects as recognized by the professors’ and the audience will be awarded by cash prize!</p>
<h3 id="rules">Rules</h3>
<ol>
<li>Interested students can register to present their projects to a panel of professors and to all visitors.</li>
<li>One registrant can only present one project at their table.</li>
<li>Registrants will be given tablespaces in the Auditorium to present their work to all visitors.</li>
<li>Students will have to use the table allotted to them. No changes will be done.</li>
<li>Students are allowed to display using posters, boards, personal laptops.</li>
<li>Students will have to place requests for monitors well in advance.</li>
<li>Students will not be provided with a schedule of when the judges might visit their table.</li>
<li>If students are not present at their table when a judge visits, the judge is well within their rights to leave.</li>
<li>In the case of the absence of students during their judges’ visit, re-evaluation requests will be entertained.</li>
</ol>
<h3 id="eligibility">Eligibility</h3>
<ol>
<li>MTC members present at the project expo for free.</li>
<li>Non-MTC members present after paying a price of 5 AED.</li>
</ol>

<h3 id="voting-procedure">Voting Procedure</h3>
<ol>
<li>Every table will be provided with a QR code.</li>
<li>If a participant would like to support the project at the table, they can scan the QR and cast their vote.</li>
<li>The votes will be collected via Forms, and MTC will handle the vote collection and counting. Edit access will not be provided.</li>
<li>In case the project with the highest vote count coincides with any of the other awards, the next highest vote count will be considered, and so on.</li>
</ol>
